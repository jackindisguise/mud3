    Module regeneration

    Regeneration system for mobs to recover health, mana, and reduce exhaustion over time.

    Mobs are added to the regeneration set when they take damage, lose mana, or gain exhaustion. The regeneration interval runs every 30 seconds and applies different rates based on combat state.
